Deber Berhan, Ethiopia

I was born in a small city of Amehara region called Deber-Berhan, Ethiopia. My Dad and my mom met in Deber Berhan city while my father was rented one of the houses near by my grandmother's house. Deber Berhan is a beautiful small city in Amehara region. It is one of the coolest palce in Ethiopia. My family used to have a herd of sheeps when I used to live there and I had one sheep by my name...lol. when my father got promotion in his job and relocated to Addis Ababa, we moved to Addis Ababa, but I have visited my birth place few times when I used to live in Ethiopia. It is my plan to visit again when I will go Ethiopia in couple of months.

Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Once we moved to Addis Ababa, we lived in the house my pateranl grandfather lived and gave to my father. I lived most of my life in Addis Ababa since the age of 4 until I went to Haramaya for collage life. Addis Ababa is the capital city of Ethiopia. I lived in differnet areas of Addis, but I had really wonderful and very memorable time my childhood village. I grew up in one of the most crowded and busy areas of the city, Merkato but everybody was nice and greatful for evertthing and everybody. Meracato is a large open-air marketplace in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, even in Africa. Most of my extended family lived near us, so I had a chance to visit them so often. Once I had an opportunity to go to college, I went to Haramaya.

Haramaya, Ethiopia

From 2005-2008 I was an undergraduate at Haramaya University,studied Computer Science. I lived in the university campus which found in East Hararghe Zone, close to Haramaya town, at about 510km East of Addis Ababa between Dire Dawa and Harar cities. since the campus provided everything for students, I didn't go out that much. The area where the campus place called Bati which was very busy area. Most Bati's people were working in the campus. I lived in the campus for three years. Luckily at the end college's year, I won Diversity Visa Lottery or DIV lottery in short. It is the opportunity United States have provided to developing contries people to come here for living, education and etc... After graduation, I went to back Addis Ababa until I finisied the travling process.

Saint Louis, MO

I came to America, United States on the hot summer, June 2009. Missouri is my first state to live on and still living in. I remebered, It was really hot and humid, I had a hard time to adjust everything, the weather, the time, the system. The first three years, I lived on south city, around downtown. I used public trasportation to move aroun td especially to go to school. I was attending some ESL(English as secondary language)clasess at St.louis community college, forest park campus. Then I moved to Florissant city when I found a job opportunity and a place to live with some family friends. I stayed there until I saved up some money and bought my first car, Toyota camery 1997. Once I got my car, it was really easy for me to move around. So, I rented my first apartment with one of my friend on North Lindbergh. I was going school parttime as well. After a few years, I moved to an other apartment on Midland with some friends. St.louis is a great palce to live for me right now, but I want move to west coast at some point in the future.
